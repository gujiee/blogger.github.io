Osmanthus fragrant, reeds turning gold,
a flute drifts long and slow — a touch of autumn settles.

The moon is round, crabs are fat,
the golden wind rustles — a cup of warm wine glows.

Mid-Autumn Festival, 2012, in the Jiangnan region.
