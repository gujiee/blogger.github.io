Once in a dream

I built a grand palace:

the blue sky for a dome,

the white sun for a lamp,

rivers and lakes for pools,

a rainbow for a bridge,

stars for inlaid jewels,

the aurora ever-shifting.

I crossed the rainbow bridge

to listen to heaven's songs,

or rode the crescent moon

on a quiet summer night

to behold the splendor of the earth.
