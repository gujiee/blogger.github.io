If there is a next life, I would be a tree.

Standing on the earth, without a trace of arrogance.

One side, the boundless lake; the other, the silent sky.

Watching white clouds drift by, year after year;
Listening to the water lap the shore, day after day.

Perfectly happy. Perfectly still.

Never reaching for more. Never begging.
