Splash, splash —

what kind of painting is this?

All colors mixed

on pure white paper —

blending, biting, devouring,

leaving behind streaks of murky tears.

What kind of painting is this?

Pure white paper,

pristine colors,

murky tears.

A great man once said:

This is life.

Written in autumn 1994

China Agricultural University
